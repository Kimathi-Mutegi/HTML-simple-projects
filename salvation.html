BEING BORN AGAIN/SALVATION

If today you were to die,would you go to heaven or hell according to God's conclusion?
Well, the Bible says that :man is destined to die once and face judgement according to Hebrews 9:27 and according to;
JOHN 3:3
Jesus answered him,“Very truly I tell you, no one can see the kingdom of God unless they are born again.”
So, if you are not born again and don't do the Father's will,you shall not enter the Kingdom of Heaven. But the good news is there is still time for you who aren't born again and still breathing.So:

1.What is salvation or being born again?

Many people confuse the meaning of being born again like in the story of Nicodemus especially those who come from other religions.These people think being born again means "entering a second time into their mother’s womb to be born." but we christians believe that Salvation or being born again simply means to be saved or delivered from the result of sin and accepting the Lord Jesus as your Savior..When Adam and Eve disobeyed God by eating from the Tree of Knowledge of good and evil , sin entered the world.This ushered making sacrifices to God. Sacrifice was intended to signify more than mere homage. The significance was that of securing forgiveness, expiation of sin, through the offering of a substitute. The offeror is not portrayed as a mere creature but specifically as a sinner, a sinful creature in need of forgiveness.But God had a plan to bring us back to Him by by sending his Only Begotten Son that whoever believes in Him,shall not perish,but have eternal life(John 3:16).Jesusalso known as The Messiah, was made sin on the cross yet he knew no sin.He is the bridge to God.Nobody gets to the Father expect through Him.

2.What are we saved from?

In the old Testament,people had the mentality that salvation means being saved from real life situations In 2 Kings 19:19, Hezekiah prays for the Lord to save them from the Assyrians. Though there are massive spiritual implications to this, the deliverance he longs for is primarily in the present. Likewise in Psalms 54:1 when David asks for God to save him he is thinking foremost about being delivered from the hand of Saul who is tracking him down. The Old Testament establishes God as the Deliverer and Savior.

In the New Testament it became,salvation occured to be deliverance : from the penalty of sin, which is eternal death (Romans 6:23) , and from mortality and given the gift of eternal life (John 3:15) .

3.By whom are we saved?

In Acts 16:30 a Philippian jailer asks a very important question. “Sir, what must I do to be saved?” The apostles did not hesitate in pointing to Jesus Christ as the only means by which men will be saved. Those in the Old Testament looked to God alone as their Savior. This is picked up in the New Testament when Jesus Christ is referred to as our Lord and Savior. God is the One who enacts salvation. The Scriptures are clear that the way of unbelief is to trust in self or in other things for salvation. Psalm 20 shows that the way of belief is to not trust in chariots or horses but to trust in the name of the Lord. We are the ones who have gotten ourselves into the mess; therefore God alone is able to save. 1 Timothy 1:15is clear that Jesus came into the world to save sinners. This means every sinner, if he/she is to be saved will be saved through Christ.

4.How do I get saved?

If you have read this article to this far,you will be wondering ,how do I get saved?
First thing you will need to note,salvation doesn't come from the works of man so that you can go around boasting but rather it is by grace you have been saved through faith.This is well explained in the book of Ephesians 2:8-9 .
ACTS 4:12
And there is salvation in no one else, for there is no other name under heaven given among men by which we must be saved.
Salvation only comes from Jesus Christ. Everyone who call upon the name of The Lord shall be saved.

Process of getting saved.

The process of getting saved in christianity is a very simple process.You pray to Jesus on the following matters:

  1. Accept that you are sinner and ask Jesus to be your personal Saviour,
  2. Believe that Jesus Christ is The Son of God and He died for your sins at the cross,
  3. Confess you sins to Jesus.

Jesus will save you if you sincerely and faithfully ask for salvation.So,please pray this short prayer in faith:
"Dear Lord Jesus, Thank you for dying on the cross for my sin. Please forgive me. Come into my life. I receive You as my Lord and Savior. Now, help me to live for you the rest of this life. In the name of Jesus, I pray. Amen."
